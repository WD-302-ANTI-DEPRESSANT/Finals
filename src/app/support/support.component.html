Baguio Mental Health Support Group

This group is created primarily for individuals with mental health issues. It is intended for people like us. We are here to give support and encouragement. We would like this group to be a safe haven for all of us and nurture an atmosphere of acceptance, respect and love. Let us all do our part to keep it that way.

Social Anxiety Support Group

Welcome! We are a peer focused support group for sufferers of anxiety, particularly social anxiety. We keep a positive atmosphere where you can feel safe to ask questions and share your experiences around social anxiety.
